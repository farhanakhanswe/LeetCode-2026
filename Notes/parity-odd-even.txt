# Parity (Even vs Odd)

# Parity tells whether a number is:

    (A) Even → divisible by 2
    (B) Odd → not divisible by 2

# Key Rules

(1) Addition
    
    even + even = even
    odd + odd = even
    even + odd = odd

(2) Subtraction
    
    even - even = even
    odd - odd = even
    even - odd = odd
    odd - even = odd

(3) Quick Trick to Remember
    
    Same parity → even result
    Different parity → odd result

(4) Code Check
    
    num % 2 === 0  // even
    num % 2 !== 0  // odd

(5) One-line summary

    Parity only depends on whether numbers are even or odd—not their actual value.
